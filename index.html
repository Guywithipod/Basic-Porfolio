About me

Hi! My name is Anthony and I'm a saint paul local, I've lived most of my life with a short break of 5 years spent in North Dakota. There I obtained my B.S. in chemical engineering at the University of North Dakota and worked for Halliburton for a little under a year. Due to the nature of Chemical engineering, most of the jobs available are factory 12 hour shifts during the night. I didn't move back just to have the same life style, I came back to be with family and friends! So after much debate I've decided that the next step was a coding boot camp course at the U of M. I'm super excited and cannot wait to begin the next chapter of my life.

A little about me on a more personal level. I don't really let stress or anger et the better of me. I'm very laid back and agreeable. I'm a people persona and I enjoy meeting new people and having them share thier stories with me! Its no surprise I work well in groups, my best work happens when I am able to bounce ideas off people. I also love helping people grow thier skills and abilities. I once had a mentor tell me I shouldn't be training people for thier current job, I should be preparing them for thier next job.
